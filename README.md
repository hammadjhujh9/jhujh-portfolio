## ğŸ“ Customization

### Update Personal Information

- **Hero Section**: Edit `components/Hero.tsx` to update name, roles, and social links
- **About Section**: Modify `components/About.tsx` with your bio
- **Skills**: Update skills in `components/Skills.tsx`
- **Projects**: Add your projects in `components/Projects.tsx`
- **Contact**: Update contact information in `components/Contact.tsx`

### Update Social Links

Edit the social links in:
- `components/Hero.tsx` (hero section)
- `components/Contact.tsx` (contact section)
- `components/Footer.tsx` (footer)

### Change Colors

Modify the color scheme in `tailwind.config.ts` under the `colors.primary` section.
